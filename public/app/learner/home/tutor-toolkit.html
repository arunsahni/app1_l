Powerful Teaching Tools.

Readiness Tests

Learn Anything helps you gauge the readiness of your students through pre-tests taken for every subject/level. This will help you analyse the students' strengths and weaknesses.

Diagnostic Results

After completion of Readiness Test, the results will be shown to the tutor. Based on the diagnostic results, you can decide how to plan the rest of the lessons with the student.

Lesson Plans

Tutors design age-appropriate lesson plans by taking reference from the MOE syllabus.

Progress Reports

Learn Anything eases parents’ worries by helping to generate progress reports that reflect the learner’s progress through the lesson journey.

  • ~ Milestone Test Report (overall improvement)
  • ~ Graded Assignments (step-by-step improvement)

Wizardry Help: Wise Owl

Our Wise Owl wizard generator helps you create teaching resources and plans from scratch! They are used to support you in the development of teaching resources and learning activities for your students.
