Tutor and Learner Ethical Service Guidelines

All Learn Anything Tutors and Learners, upon registration, have agreed to abide by the Ethical Service Guidelines to ensure both parties are protected in the course of lesson engagement. Upon violation of any of the guidelines, they will be subjected to Learn Anything’s review and charged with penalities accordingly.

Tutor’s Ethical Service Guidelines

  1. Tutor will maintain sound mental and physical health to perform his/her duties. Tutor will not be disrespectful or behave in a derogatory way towards the Learner

    1. Verbal abuse: vulgar name-calling, mocking, insulting

    2. Physical abuse or sexual harassment

  2. Tutor will not expose Learner to embarrassment and disparagement

  3. Tutor will not divulge private and confidential information given by Tutor to third parties unless consented by Learner or Learn Anything Pte Ltd

  4. Tutor will bear responsibility to protect Learner from conditions detrimental to health, learning and safety if:

    1. If tuition is not held at home, but in a public place

    2. If there is no parent/guardian present in the tuition space except for yourself

  5. Tutor will take full responsibility of teaching the Learner and must not send replacement tutors on his/her behalf

  6. Tutor will not use his/her professional relationship with Learner for reasons other than advancing the learning experience with Learner

  7. Tutor will not use the professional relationship with Learner to his/her advantage by conducting or initiating other business / commercial activities during lessons time.

  8. Tutor will not provide false teaching experience and/or qualifications information to Learn Anything or the Learner. He/she is held accountable to all credentials he/her has listed

  9. Tutor will not discriminate Learner based on any of these grounds: racial, gender, marital status, sexual orientation, social and/or cultural differences.

  10. Tutor will provide top-notch teaching service, adding to the learning experience any of the aforementioned learning practices and methods as listed in his/her teaching profile

  11. Tutor will only use his/her own teaching material, public texts (textbooks and worksheets), and/or the resources provided by Learn Anything. He/she will not infringe on another’s Intellectual property or claim another’s resources as his/her own

  12. Tutor will not threaten, bribe, force or harass Learner to continue or renew his/her services in the event the lesson package has ended or is discontinued.

  13. Tutor will honor any special/miscellaneous teaching agreement or arrangement that was agreed upon at time of engagement with Learner:

    1. As discussed during the video interview

    2. As discussed in electronic mail

    3. As discussed in other communication platforms

    Upon violation of this, Tutor will be required to show evidence of botched contract as proof.

  14. Tutor will be penalised by Learn Anything for continuous cancellation or rescheduling that involves multiple/severe inconveniencing to the Learner, even if it is done during the grace period.


    Learner can cancel the lesson package with no penalty charges incurred if Tutor violates any of the ethical service guidelines listed above.
